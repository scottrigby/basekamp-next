Hi everyone,

Tomorrow we’ll be talking with Bryce Dwyer from InCUBATE and their current resident, Christopher Kennedy, about similar research they are doing & have done recently.

**About InCUBATE & Bryce Dwyer’s research:**

InCUBATE is an experimental research institute and residency program operating out of a shared storefront space in Chicago. InCUBATE’s latest project is the Artist Run Credit League (ARCL - [http://artistruncreditleague.com/](http://artistruncreditleague.com/ "http://artistruncreditleague.com/")) — a still-forming rotating credit association for artist run groups and spaces in Chicago.

This summer Bryce, one of the members of InCUBATE, researched unconventional residency programs around the world and assembled a provisional database of them. These programs are often not non-profits, organize themselves in novel ways, and operate out of idiosyncratic spaces.

[http://incubate-chicago.org](http://incubate-chicago.org "http://incubate-chicago.org")

**About Artiscycle, Christopher Kennedy’s research:**

Artiscycle Platform: A portal for art groups and spaces. A tool for educators, artists and citizens.  
The Artiscycle platform will be a collaborative opensource database of artist spaces, groups and projects. The website will help participants understand the organizational models and modes of engagement used by art spaces and groups; a resource for educators, artists and citizens to collaborate and find inspiration for projects and learning strategies in their community.

More here: [http://www.artiscycle.net/platform](http://www.artiscycle.net/platform "http://www.artiscycle.net/platform")
