Hi everyone,

As a follow-up to [last week’s multi-city chat](http://basekamp.com/about/events/occupy-everything "http://basekamp.com/about/events/occupy-everything"), this Tuesday we’re again asking people from cities who are or want to be involved in the wider #Occupy movement to continue to connect and share.

We will be online live from Occupy Philadelphia and Occupy Chicago, creating an opening for anyone present to speak. If you are at an Occupy site in another city and wish to join in, please let us know, or just show up on Skype or IRC. Starting @ 6PM EST, and (at least in Philadelphia) continuing through the General Assembly.

## If you want to share, Join us this Tuesday

- Add your voice on Skype (skypename: ‘basekamp’)
- Or join in person if you happen to be in Philadelphia, THIS WEEK AGAIN we’ll be broadcasting at City Hall for Occupy Philadelphia, at the Tech Tent powerd by solar panels!
- Or join in person if you happen to be in Chicago, at @ La Salle and Jackson, we will have a roving, possibly flakey phone broadcast!
- If you prefer text chat, we are also on IRC (on Tuesday and before): [http://webchat.freenode.net/?channels=occupyeverything](http://webchat.freenode.net/?channels=occupyeverything "http://webchat.freenode.net/?channels=occupyeverything")

See you all then!
