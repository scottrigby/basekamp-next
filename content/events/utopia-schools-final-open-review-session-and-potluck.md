**Where:** Flux Factory, 39-31 29th Street, Long Island City, Queens, New York 11101

Utopia School is a collaborative schoolhouse we’re creating to study past and present Utopian projects, while working towards new ones.

The process of building the school is a movement towards creating a utopian experiment of our own. Recipe for disaster? You tell us!

Please join us, in person or via Skype for a potluck dinner and the last\* chance to pitch your class, installation, field trip, song, dance, or other ideas.

After this potluck we’re going to be working our buns off to make the calendar, and facilitate all these awesome classes, so get your ideas a flowing and come meet us!

If you would like to participate via skype, we will start the conversation around 7:30 pm EST.

Cheers!

∞Utopia School∞

——————————————————————————-

\* “Final” and “Last” are relative terms in our project. Always feel free to pitch something or join in, even if a deadline has passed.
