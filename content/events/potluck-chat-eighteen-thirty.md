Hi everyone,  
This week we’ll be talking with Suzy & Sam from Eighteen Thirty about their projects & ideas.

Short description:

> Eighteen Thirty Collaborations (ETC.) is a collective of inspired individuals who join together and break apart to organize and undertake various performances, publications, and ambitious collaborations. Originally conceptualized as a project space for performative works, Eighteen Thirty Collaborations has since challenged itself to step outside of its thick walls and continue to organize in outside organizations and in the public sphere.  If we get lost for a while, assume we are huddled under a bridge somewhere talking big about the city, space, and sand in the hourglass.
