Hi everyone,  
This week we’re having an informal chat with Brandon Joyce from PIFAS about his recent lecture tour on Drugs and Madness.

Brandon will come to discuss drugs and madness as _dialects of experience_. That is, as worldviews that, despite any question of health, utility, or deceny, are still meaningful orders deserving of epistemological dignity and— if possible— a kind of _translation_ into common intelligibility. Or something like that. There’s more to say, but let’s say it in person!
