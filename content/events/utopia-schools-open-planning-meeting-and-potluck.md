Hi again,

After a long slumber, we’re ready to share some of what we’ve been working on over the past year.

Come join us, in person or by Skype, to brainstorm the curricula for Utopia School, meet and greet one another, and eat a delicious meal.

Utopia School is a temporary schoolhouse we’re creating to share information about spaces and initiatives that re-imagine the world in some crucial way. The first session of Utopia School will take place at Flux Factory in October, 2014.

This open meeting is all about gathering together initial collaborators, ideas and resources from near and far. We’ll start the in-person potluck at 7 PM ET, and start the Skype conversation around 8:30 PM ET (if you haven’t joined past Skype chats, [here’s how](http://basekamp.com/skype "http://basekamp.com/skype")).

This meeting is OPEN to the public, so invite people as you like :) Oh yeah, and if you come in person, bring a dish!
