**Where:** Luncheonette 221 Nassau Avenue @ North Henry Street, Greenpoint, Brooklyn 11222

Come join us for the first open review meeting and potluck to look over proposals for the October 2014 session of Utopia School. This is one of four open review sessions where we will gather, eat, and start to address some of the actualities behind the classes, installations, field trips and other explorative measures we’re cooking up for the Utopia School.

We’re working with multiple deadlines because we want to build the school in phases, with sessions of reviewing proposals together as a chance to build upon those classes in person, and also to allow for new proposals to sprout from the ones reviewed in each round.

This is an OPEN meeting. You can come even if you have not submitted anything yet (we can create proposals in person at this meeting), or even if it’s the first time you’ve heard of the project and you just want to check it out.  Please bring some food to share if you can, but if you can’t just bring yourself.

We look forward to seeing you!

- The Utopia School Team
